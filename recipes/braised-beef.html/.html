Ghanaian Braised Beef

Braised beef is a deeply seasoned and tender dish cooked slowly in a rich sauce made from local spices, tomatoes, onions, and herbs. The beef is first browned to seal in flavor, then simmered gently in a spicy, aromatic mixture that brings out the essence of Ghanaian cooking. This flavorful dish is perfect with rice, boiled yam, plantain, or banku. It’s a comfort meal that combines traditional taste with a soft, melt-in-the-mouth texture.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or palm oil
  • 2 large onions (1 chopped, 1 blended)
  • 3 medium tomatoes (blended)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves (pepre)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika or suya spice
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme (fresh or dried)
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper (blended or chopped)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup beef stock or water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef: Wash and cut beef into medium chunks. Season with salt, black pepper, ginger, garlic, cloves, and suya spice. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes
  2. Sear the beef: Heat oil in a pot. Brown the beef on all sides to lock in flavor, then remove and set aside.
  3. Make the base sauce: In the same pot, add chopped onions and fry until golden. Add tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes. Then add the blended tomatoes, blended onion, and scotch bonnet pepper. Cook until the sauce thickens and the oil begins to separate.
  4. Add spices: Stir in curry powder, thyme, and the seasoning cube. Mix well.
  5. Add the beef: Return the browned beef to the pot, stir to coat it well with the sauce.
  6. Add liquid: Pour in beef stock or water until it just covers the meat. Add bay leaf, then cover and cook on low heat for 2–3 hours, stirring occasionally.
  7. Finish cooking: Once the beef is tender and the sauce is rich and thick, taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve: Serve hot with boiled rice, yam, plantain, or any preferred side dish.
