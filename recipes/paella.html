Spanish Paella

Traditional Spanish Paella

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 450 g (1 lb) chicken thighs or drumsticks, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 200 g (7 oz) chorizo sausage, sliced (optional but adds great flavor)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, grated or finely chopped
  • 1½ cups (300 g) paella rice (e.g., Bomba, Calasparra, or Arborio rice)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp saffron threads (or 1 tsp turmeric as a cheaper alternative)
  • 4 cups (1 liter) chicken or seafood stock, warm
  • 1 cup (240 ml) dry white wine (optional but traditional)
  • 250 g (9 oz) large shrimp (prawns), peeled and deveined
  • 250 g (9 oz) mussels or clams, scrubbed clean
  • 1 cup (150 g) frozen peas
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley and lemon wedges, for garnish
